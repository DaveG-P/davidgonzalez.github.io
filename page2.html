About me

Hi!

my name is David, and I am from Bogota Colombia. I grew up in Miami and have called Berkeley my home for the past 8 years. I have a background in Cognitive Science and Data Analytics. After a couple of years working in research & thinking about human behavior, I decided to go back to my earliest passions, Art! With an interest in psychology, design and technology, I decided to enroll at Berkeley City College to pursue my new dream of becoming a UX & UI designer.

Interests

When I am not busy working or adulting, I love to go on adventures. Whether they are urban hiking around San Francisco, riding my bike around the bay or planning trips, I love to go out and get lost. Before COVID I loved spending my time in Coffee Shops, going to local galleries and trying all kinds of beer at breweries. I also love listening to a lot of Music and following my favorite bands. I am currently listening to a lot of late 70’s and 80’s punk and indie bands such as Joy Division, Sonic Youth, The Smiths etc. With the pandemic going on, I spend a lot of time these days meditating, taking care of my plants and catching up on a lot of reading.
